Vulcan

Roman origin. God of the Smithy and Metalworks, based off the Greek Hephaestus. Son of Juno, who bore him alone without a male partner; mythology says she either found him too ugly or disliked the fact he was disabled (he is depicted as a cripple), and she threw him out the window into the ocean, where he was rescued by Thetis. Husband to Venus, the goddess of Love and Beauty, a marriage that is presented roughly with neither particularly happy about the arrangement.
