Rhea

Greek origin. A titan, daughter of Ouranos (Uranus) and Gaia, sister and wife of Cronus (Cronos) and mother to the gods and goddesses: Zeus, Hera, Hestia, Hades, Poseidon, and Demeter. Cronus was told one of his children would overpower him, so he swallowed each as Rhea gave birth and presented them to him. When Rhea had Zeus, she smuggled him away to safety and instead gave Cronus a bundle of rocks, which he swallowed like all the rest and was left none the wiser, until Zeus returned and deposed him.
