Juno

Roman origin. Goddess of Marriage and Childbirth, based off of the Greek Hera. Sometimes titled as ‘the queen of the sky’ and wife of Jupiter, mother of Mars and Vulcan. Like her Greek counterpart, Juno and Jupiter’s relationship was often strained, and many of the Greek myths were adopted into the Roman versions of the gods’ lives.
