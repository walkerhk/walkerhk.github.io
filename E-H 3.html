Hera

Greek origin. Goddess of Marriage and Childbirth. Daughter of Rhea and Cronus (Cronos), sister and wife of Zeus, and mother of Ares, Hebe, Hephaestus, and Eileithyia. She was swallowed by Cronus when born, like her siblings Poseidon, Hades, Demeter, and Hestia; they were all rescued when Zeus made Cronus vomit them up, and she eventually married him.
